Training at home

How it works

Provided you live near or in between the markers on the map, I will come to your home and train you. When you feel confident to, we will use the baby in the sessions too. As you will come to realise pretty early on, early motherhood takes a real toll on your body. It is common for your lower back to compensate for the constant bending and picking up baby. Your posture is likely to take a hit, from the cuddling and breastfeeding ( I am not a breastfeeding preacher, "breast" is not Always best, FED is though!). We use the baby in sessions to prepare and strengthen your body to combat the strains on everyday mum life. Plus, your baby is a free weight, which gets heavier as you get stronger!
