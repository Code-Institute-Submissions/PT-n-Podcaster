I have a strict 24 hour cancellation policy. If you would like to rearrange a session, please give me a minimum of 24 hours notice. If you do not comply with this rule, you will lose your session. • Please pay your package total upfront, if this is not possible, you and I can workout a payment plan. I will not accept payment per session. • If you purchase a package and cannot complete the sessions for any reason, I will not refund payment of unused sessions UNLESS there are extenuating circumstances. I will require proof, for example a Doctors note/ new work contract, stipulating a relocation. HOWEVER your sessions can be frozen for up to 3 months. *All decisions and approaches to moving forward in any of the above circumstances are at my discretion. • If you decide to go for an outdoor package, please understand that we will more than likely experience bad weather. If this will deter you from training this is not the right package for you. I will not accept bad weather as a reason for a cancelled session. • Please come to every session with a pro-active attitude. If you cant do an exercise, know that you are struggling AT THE MOMENT but you WILL improve.

Podcaster

How I can help you

Podcasting empowered me. Whilst I had no trouble finding or using my voice... The actual production of my first podcast was very daunting to me! Whether you want a guest, host, a soundboard for your ideas, editing advice and support or just an ear to listen, I'm your girl! I'm offering these services because Podcasting has given me so much joy, but so many times I nearly gave up because of all the little niggly bits in between, I am trying to be the person to minimise those obstacles for you so that you can get to your voice out there as soon as possible!
