Podcasting Journey

My podcasting journey began when I founded the Tried it podcast.

I felt that there were not enough conversations about things I could relate too. I realised that, this must be the case for other women eho looked like me too!

I started putting the feelers out ther, using my instagram to spread the word that I was on the hunt for a few women who also shared my vision. Eventually, Kedeisha Jade and I got together and Tried It podcast was born.

We started recording weekly and sooner or later, we were getting feedback from other girls who loved listening to us. I was overwhelmed by how many women who could relate to my experiences reached out. I felt like I was becoming the representation I would have loved to see when I was growing up.

Eventually, I was asked to be a guest on other podcasts! This was even more invigorating, because it had meant that people had recognised what I do and thought it was so great that they wanted to put it on their platforms! The idea that my perspective on things was reaching a wider range of people really excited me.

THEN, Tried It Podcast came to an end, I was heartbroken because I hought that this meant that was it for me and podcasting.. But NO. I realsied that Podcasting was a true and pure passion of mine. I needed to continue at all costs!! I started my own podcast Mayas minutes and conitued popping up on other peoples' episodes.
