Training at home

How it works

We live in the modern age. Women are powerhouses and your schedule should not get in the way of your fitness! I can carry out sessions via facetime zoom or whatsapp videa chat. This became my most popular method of training women during COVID for obvious reasons.. Plus, it empowered many of my clients and women world wide to get fit on their own terms. I feel that there is so much pressure surrounding women and their bodies. Unfortunately, health and fitness, is presented to us as a prized and priveleged behaviour/ lifestyle which can only be carried out in one way (usually in expensive gyms). COVID and lockdown really showed that everyone has their own style and when the pressure to do things in a certain way is removed, it makes way for people to find THEIR way. I will train you from your living room/ bedroom or garden and it'll feel like im right their with you!
